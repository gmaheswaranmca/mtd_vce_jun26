# Sliding Window Learning Order

## Phase 1: Fixed Size Window

1. **643** - Maximum Average Subarray I
2. **1456** - Maximum Number of Vowels in a Substring of Given Length
3. **1343** - Number of Sub-arrays of Size K and Average Greater than or Equal to Threshold

---

## Phase 2: Basic Variable Size Window

4. **209** - Minimum Size Subarray Sum
5. **713** - Subarray Product Less Than K
6. **1493** - Longest Subarray of 1's After Deleting One Element

---

## Phase 3: Character Frequency Window

7. **3** - Longest Substring Without Repeating Characters
8. **159** - Longest Substring with At Most Two Distinct Characters
9. **340** - Longest Substring with At Most K Distinct Characters
10. **424** - Longest Repeating Character Replacement

---

## Phase 4: Anagram Pattern

11. **438** - Find All Anagrams in a String
12. **567** - Permutation in String

---

## Phase 5: Advanced Variable Window

13. **1004** - Max Consecutive Ones III
14. **904** - Fruit Into Baskets
15. **1838** - Frequency of the Most Frequent Element

---

## Phase 6: Substring Coverage Problems

16. **76** - Minimum Window Substring
17. **727** - Minimum Window Subsequence

---

## Phase 7: Monotonic Queue Window

18. **239** - Sliding Window Maximum
19. **862** - Shortest Subarray with Sum at Least K
20. **1438** - Longest Continuous Subarray With Absolute Diff Less Than or Equal to Limit

---

## Phase 8: Hard Sliding Window

21. **992** - Subarrays with K Different Integers
22. **1234** - Replace the Substring for Balanced String
23. **1358** - Number of Substrings Containing All Three Characters
24. **76** - Minimum Window Substring (Revisit)
